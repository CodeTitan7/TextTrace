About TextTrace

TextTrace is a detection application for content aimed at helping individuals trace content developed by chatbots easily. It can take a snippet of text, which goes through a machine learning algorithm that has been trained on enormous data of human-written and AI-developed text. TextTrace, based on research, provides a confidence score in which users are provided with a better idea of the likelihood of AI involvement in writing.



TextTrace is an effective tool and has its significance in a variety of fields. Education is the place where it verifies authenticity of essays and assignments through AI-detection. In terms of content verification, it supports journalists, researchers, and business enterprises to discover AI-generated content, such as articles, blogs, and social media content. It also aids marketers and SEO specialists through giving authenticity and human goodness to content. TextTrace provides you with a reliable tool to verify text with ease.
